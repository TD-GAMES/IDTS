About


IDTS is a free, fanmade arcade racing game based on Initial D Stage 1. It recreates
the intense downhill battles with a visual style inspired by the Legend movie trilogy.

This game is a non-profit passion project. Everything
from models to physics is created by the community.



Choose Your Ride


Choose from iconic cars straight out of the Initial D anime and hit the mountain passes

The game is currently in development. Not all cars are included yet,
but new ones are introduced frequently through ongoing updates.



Gamemodes


Race through multiple game modes:


  • Story Mode: Race against AI opponents based on characters from the Initial D anime.
  • Time Attack: Chase the fastest times on every mountain pass.
  • Custom Race Mode: Create your own custom races with personalized settings, opponents, and conditions.
